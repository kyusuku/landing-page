The Four Fundamental Forces of The Universe

Have you ever watched Marvel's The Eternals? The four fundamental forces of the universe are just like them ― they exists to keep the whole universe running properly. Without these four forces, the universe would collapse, and our understanding of the Standard Model would not exist. The four fundamental forces consist of gravity, electromagnetic force, strong nuclear force, and weak nuclear force
